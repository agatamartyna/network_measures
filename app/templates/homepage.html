Welcome to Network Measures app

This device calculates basic parametres of the structure of the lexicon: the degree (i.e. phonological neighbourhood density) and the clustering coefficient. It was designed and created for research purposes for phonologists, psychologists, and psycholinguists. Follow the instructions to generate your desired output.
